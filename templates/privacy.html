Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

1. Data We Collect

Spendly collects information you provide directly when you use the service. This includes your account credentials (username and email address), expense entries (amounts, categories, dates, descriptions), and basic usage data such as login timestamps. We do not collect any sensitive financial information such as bank account numbers, credit card details, or social security numbers.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data exclusively to provide and improve the expense tracking service. Specifically, we use your information to: create and manage your account, store and categorize your expense entries, generate spending summaries and analytics, and personalize your experience. We do not use your personal data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in our database hosted by a trusted infrastructure provider. We employ standard security practices including encrypted connections and access controls. While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. We retain your data for as long as your account remains active.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. Google Fonts is loaded to display typography on the website. Your browser communicates with Google's servers when fonts are fetched. These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing Google's privacy policy for details on how they handle your data.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, how your data is handled, or wish to request deletion of your account and associated data, please contact us through the application or visit our repository on GitHub. We will respond to legitimate privacy requests within a reasonable timeframe.
